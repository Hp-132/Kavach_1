Safety Tips

  • Keep your smart torch charged and within reach.
  • Verify your emergency contacts every month.
  • Use the high-decibel siren if you feel you are being followed.
  • The flashlight can be used to momentarily blind an attacker at night.
  • Always inform a trusted person about your travel route.
